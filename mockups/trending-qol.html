RECOMMENDATIONS — TRENDING TAB

TRENDING — QoL ROADMAP

Things I'd add to make Trending feel like a tool you reach for daily, not a dashboard you check once a week. Tagged by effort and impact so you can pick what to build first.

HIGHEST IMPACT FIRST

01 Bulk select + download EFFORT SIMPACT HIGH

Hold Cmd/Shift to multi-select reels in the grid, then a floating action bar offers Download all selected, Add to collection, or Export stats CSV. Most-asked feature in similar tools — once you start tracking, you want to grab a batch at once, not one at a time.

02 New-post desktop notifications EFFORT SIMPACT HIGH

When a tracked creator posts — especially a trial reel, since those decay fast — pop a desktop notification. Click takes you straight to the post in the viewer. Per-creator + per-event-type toggles in settings (e.g. notify only for trial reels, not regular posts).

03 Hook & caption library EFFORT MIMPACT HIGH

Auto-extract the first 5 words ("the hook") of every post your tracked creators publish, ranked by view performance. Filter by creator or tag. Click any hook to see the full caption + post. Use this hook in editor button paths back into the editor with the text pre-filled. Turns competitor research into reusable templates.

SMALL WINS

11 "Use this in editor" deep link EFFORT SIMPACT MED

Button on any tracked post: opens the editor with the image loaded as background and the caption pre-filled. Lets you riff on a competitor's post in 1 click instead of manual download → drag → retype.

Caption preview:
"POV: you just got off the plane and your hotel is across the canal"
→ OPEN IN EDITOR
12 Webview connection status indicator EFFORT SIMPACT MED

Small status dot in the Trending header — green = IG session is alive, yellow = stale (refresh in <1h), red = expired (click to re-login via webview). No more silent failures because cookies expired.

TRENDING IG · CONNECTED

13 Caption + hashtag clipboard buttons EFFORT SIMPACT MED

Tiny copy buttons in the post viewer for the full caption AND just the hashtags. One-tap to your clipboard. Common need; saves the manual select-all dance.

"how I fold a button-up so it never wrinkles in a carry-on #linen #travel #cairo #ootd"
