① CLICK / TAPbutton presses, toggles, list selection — fired on most interactions

Pebble
Soft sine plop. Quiet, friendly, doesn't fatigue over a long session.
Tick
Crisp mechanical. Like a typewriter key — feels precise and tactile.
Tap
Tiny upward pitch chirp. Modern app-y, similar to Apple's tap feedback.
Bubble
Round downward pop. Playful — leans toward consumer apps over pro tools.

② SUCCESS / CONFIRMexport complete, save committed, restore done — positive completion

Chime
Three-note major arpeggio (C-E-G). Clear "done" feeling without being saccharine.
Lift
Single note sliding upward. Subtle and elegant — used on Save / OK actions.
Bell
Resonant ding with harmonic shimmer. Clean, slightly classical.
Coin
Two-beat "ding-ding". Game-y. Use sparingly — fun for one-off rewards.

③ ERROR / FAILexport failed, file rejected, network error — negative without being harsh

Drop
Pitch slides from high to low. Clear "that didn't work" without being aggressive.
Buzz
Short sawtooth grunt. Warning-tone, like a soft buzzer.
Donk
Single dull thump. Very polite — feels like a shake-of-the-head.
Nope
Two-note minor descending interval. Subtle "no" without being a klaxon.

④ NOTIFICATIONtoast appears, update available, background event — informational

Bloop
Soft midrange round tone. Quiet "fyi" — doesn't demand attention.
Ping
Bright high sine. Notification-app classic. Catches your eye without being loud.
Chirp
Quick upward sweep. Bird-like, friendly, energetic.
Thud
Low sine bump. Tactile — "something just landed". Good for downloads finishing.
